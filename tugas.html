    Business Process Measure Tool

    In addition can be measured by the output or output of the process, business process performance can be measured from the result or its input. But the quality of the output of a business process is not only influenced by the process itself, but also influenced by other factors such as input and business environment.

    Customer Perspective

    If the customer feels that the product or service he or she buys is acceptable on time, the quality and specifications satisfy his or her desires and the cost is relatively low as promised in the brochure or offer, the company must have performed the business process correctly, using the correct method.

    Perspective Internal Process

    If the company employees feel the work process is not too bureaucratic, the acceptance of the decision can be done quickly and clearly and all activities can be explored clearly if there is an error to attempt to make improvements, then the company has had the right business process design that resulted in internal processes Business become good.

    Perspective Supplier

    If a company as a vendor of another company feels that dealing with a particular company as its customer then the company is very clear in formulating the desire or needs to be fulfilled, including in terms of specifications, delivery time and price rational and matter of payment and billing appointments Then surely the company already has the right business process. If the three stakeholders are customers, employees and suppliers have felt good benefits associated with the company. Then that is the measure of the success of a business process utilization.

    Shareholders

    If all three stakeholders mentioned above have benefited then another important stakeholder is shareholders definitely will also benefit, because the process is working well and smoothly will definitely increase productivity and income company, and this will certainly increase the value of company.
